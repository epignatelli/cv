I defended the thesis “Computational morphogenesis and construction of an acoustic shell for outdoor chamber music”.
Implementing numerical methods that uses geometrical acoustics, computational physics, descriptive geometry and genetic algorithms, the thesis presents a novel generatsive method to design a passive acoustic shell for outdoor classical music.
